Case
A user‑specific record containing all documents, stages, communications, and compliance artifacts.

Stage
One of 14 regulated steps required for withdrawal completion.

Countdown
A compliance enforcement mechanism that resets the withdrawal pathway if deadlines are missed.

Reactivation
A controlled process to restore access after countdown expiry.
