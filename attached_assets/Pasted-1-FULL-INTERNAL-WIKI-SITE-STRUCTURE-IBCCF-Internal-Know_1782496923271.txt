5. FULL INVESTOR Q&A PACK
IBCCF Investor Question Bank — Version 1.0
1. Product & Technology
Q: What problem does IBCCF solve?  
A: It standardizes compliance, documentation, and withdrawal processes in digital‑asset environments.

Q: What makes the platform unique?  
A: Immutable compliance artifacts, countdown enforcement, and full auditability.

2. Market & Competition
Q: Who are your competitors?  
A: Traditional case‑management tools, none of which offer regulated withdrawal flows or immutable compliance.

Q: What is your market size?  
A: The global digital‑asset compliance market is expanding rapidly due to regulatory pressure.

3. Business Model
Q: How do you make money?  
A: SaaS licensing, per‑case fees, compliance automation, enterprise support, and white‑label licensing.

Q: What is your margin profile?  
A: High gross margins due to software‑driven operations.

4. Risk & Compliance
Q: How do you handle regulatory risk?  
A: Through immutable logs, mandatory documentation, and multi‑jurisdiction compliance mapping.

Q: How do you protect user data?  
A: RBAC, encryption, no local storage, GDPR alignment.

5. Growth & Roadmap
Q: What’s next for IBCCF?  
A: AI‑driven compliance automation, enterprise integrations, and global expansion.

Q: What is your scaling strategy?  
A: Modular architecture, autoscaling infrastructure, and jurisdiction‑specific compliance modules.
