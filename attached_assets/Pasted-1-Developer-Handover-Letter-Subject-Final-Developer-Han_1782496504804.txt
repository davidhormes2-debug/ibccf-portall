Core Compliance Controls
Immutable Timeline: All case actions are permanently logged.

Declaration of Compliance: Digitally signed, immutable, and stored in read‑only format.

NDA Integrity Sweep: Automated daily verification of NDA compliance.

Withdrawal Reset Logic: Enforced when countdown expires.

Document Validation: KYC, SOF, FATCA, and receipts validated and logged.
