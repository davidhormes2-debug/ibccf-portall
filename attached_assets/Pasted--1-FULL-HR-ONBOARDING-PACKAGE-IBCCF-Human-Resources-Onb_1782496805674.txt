---

## **B. Stage Explanation Script**
“You are currently on Stage [X] of the withdrawal process.  
This stage requires: [requirements].  
Once completed, the system will automatically advance you to the next stage.”

---

## **C. Countdown Activation Script**
“A countdown has been activated on your case due to incomplete compliance requirements.  
Please complete the pending actions before the timer expires to avoid a reset.”

---

## **D. Countdown Expiry Script**
“Your countdown has expired.  
Your withdrawal pathway has been reset in accordance with compliance policy.  
To restore access, please complete the reactivation step shown in your portal.”

---

## **E. Reactivation Script**
“To restore access, please upload the reactivation receipt.  
Once approved, your portal access will be reinstated.”

---

## **F. No‑Promises Script**

All progress is determined by the system based on completed requirements.”
